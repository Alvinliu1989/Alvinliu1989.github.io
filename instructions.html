Instructions for sharing KeyShotXR

Below is your KeyShotXR. To view the KeyShotXR by itself open the WaveSandal.408.html file in the WaveSandal.408 folder.

Upload the 'WaveSandal.408’ folder with all of its contents to your webserver. Copy (Ctrl-C/Cmd-C) and Paste (Ctrl-V/Cmd-V) the code below to your webpage.

  </div>

</div>
